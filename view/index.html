About KUSEC
Introduction

KUSEC is the only private economic organization working exclusively with the US, and it is Korea’s counterpart to the fifty federal governments of the US.

Detail View →
History

The Korea-US Economic Council (KUSEC) was founded in 1973 under "Article 26 for the Establishment of Non-profit Organizations" of the Economic Planning Board (predecessor to Ministry of Economy and Finance).
